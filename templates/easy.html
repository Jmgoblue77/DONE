DONE. is extremely easy to use. Simply follow the below guidelines.

For short term to-dos:

  1. Enter your to-do.

  2. Fill in the prompts of when, where, and how you will begin your to-do.

  3. Get back a concrete plan for starting your to-do.

  4. See the results!

For long term goals:

  1. Enter your goal.

  2. Create a few if-then plans for potential obstacles.

  3. Create short term to-dos for your goal.

  4. See the results!
